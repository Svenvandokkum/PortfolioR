An Analytical mind with a passion for Technology

My drive for studying is fueled by a curiousity for stuff that really interests me. Things that really get me going are real-time algorithms for sensors, practical use of mathematics, designing and training an AI. AI specifically interest me both in the technical details, such as how to optimize a neural network, and in a more practical sense, such as what data augmentations are best in a specific setting. In my next years of studying I want to continue growing my analytical skills and practical skills in technology. Since 2023 I have been pursuing my Bachelor's in Creative Technology and I have broadened my knowledge significantly. In the upcoming year I will start specializing myself by doing my master's.

Master's

These programs have a lot of overlap, so I hope that I can both become an applied mathematician with practical insights and a roboticist with an analytical perspective.
